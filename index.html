Bay Area Rep Rap Presents: All Things 3D Printing

Bay Area Rep Rap members have been sharing [ideas, tips, best term for concept here please] at local meetings since the earliest days of “repetative rapid prototyping,” aka 3D printing. The group has people who build their own 3D printers to those who are just starting on a creative journey. Just like you, all of us had to start knowing nothing; learning by looking for answers to questions.

Answers? The web is strewn with videos, tips and tutorials, so much information is overwhelming, especially when you want a simple answer to what you thought was a simple question.

For now, this guide will point you to our favorite answers to common questions we hear, to be replaced with more and more answers coming from our members as we build our knowledge base.

Take a look at our Bay Area Rep Rap Discussion Group and feel free to post your questions.

We meet in San Jose (TheShop.build) twice monthly – please drop by if you can… even bring your printer!
