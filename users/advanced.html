Upgrading the Firmware

Most of the current 3D printers in the hobbyist market run on an Arduino microcontroller based main board. This makes modifying the firmware as easy as downloading the Arduino IDE and the firmware source, making your modifications and uploading it to your printer via USB. Thomas Sanladerer has a nice video howto on upgrading the marlin firmware

Automatic Bed Leveling

Getting the bed level is essential for good prints, and a real pain on printers that do not have this feature. You have to fiddle with knobs on the corners of a heated build plate, using feeler gauges (or folded papaer you hope is the right thickness). There are several vendors who are happy to sell you sensors and most of the modern firmwares support using those sensors to provide the feature (with possibly some recompilation).
